About The Event

Geek Challenge is an event that will take place on April 29-30 at FSEGS. It is a 24-hour development competition for teams of up to three students, offering a unique opportunity to test their skills and learn from experienced instructors. Beyond coding, students can also gain valuable marketing, organization, and project management skills. Through collaboration, they can explore new technologies and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems. It's an excellent chance to develop critical skills needed tomake their mark in the tech world.
